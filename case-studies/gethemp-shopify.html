About GetHemp

GetHemp is a UK-based CBD and hemp products retailer offering a wide range of high-quality products including CBD flower, gummies, cartridges, oils, balms, and plant-based supplements. The brand positions itself as a quality-first CBD retailer in a market that has grown rapidly but is still navigating consumer trust, regulatory compliance, and the ongoing challenge of distinguishing genuine quality products from lower-grade alternatives.

GetHemp serves customers across the United Kingdom with fast next-day delivery available on orders and an ongoing discount structure that rewards both new and returning customers. The breadth of their product range, spanning multiple CBD formats and consumption methods, requires a well-organised store structure that helps customers find the right product for their specific needs.

The Trust and Compliance Challenge in CBD eCommerce

CBD eCommerce in the UK operates in a heavily regulated environment with specific requirements around product claims, labelling, and the way products can be described. Selling CBD products online also requires navigating significant consumer scepticism: many potential customers are curious about CBD but uncertain about quality, legality, and whether a specific product is right for their situation.

GetHemp needed a Shopify store that communicated product quality and brand credibility immediately, organised a large and diverse product catalogue in a way that made navigation intuitive, surfaced the delivery proposition prominently since speed of delivery is a significant purchase driver in this category, and complied with UK CBD marketing guidelines throughout.

The Store Architecture and Design

AspireNet approached the GetHemp store design with a clean, credible aesthetic that moves the brand away from the visual clichés common in the CBD category. The goal was a store that communicated quality and professionalism first, with the product category being evident but not the sole visual focus.

The navigation structure was given particular attention given the breadth of the product catalogue. Clear top-level categories allow customers to navigate directly to the product type they are looking for, whether that is CBD flower, oils, gummies, or vape products, without being overwhelmed by the full range on first arrival.

The free shipping threshold and the next-day delivery proposition were surfaced prominently across the store, as these are two of the strongest conversion drivers in the UK CBD market. Customers who know they can receive their order the next day and hit a free shipping threshold are significantly more likely to complete a purchase than those who are uncertain about fulfilment.

Technical Configuration and Compliance

The GetHemp store was built on Shopify with an age verification integration appropriate for regulated product categories. Product descriptions were written and structured in compliance with UK CBD marketing guidelines, ensuring that the store could continue operating without regulatory issues as the UK CBD market continues to develop its compliance framework.

The promotional structure including the discount code system and free shipping threshold was implemented through Shopify's native functionality, allowing the brand team to manage promotions and loyalty incentives without ongoing developer involvement. The store structure also accommodates the regular addition of new products as GetHemp expands its range.

Outcomes and Brand Position

The GetHemp Shopify store gives the brand a professional, credible digital presence in a market where trust is the primary purchase barrier and where the visual and structural quality of the store is directly correlated with the customer's willingness to buy from a brand they have not purchased from before.

The organised product catalogue, prominent delivery proposition, and clean design language combine to create a store that converts curious first-time visitors into confident buyers. For a brand competing in a crowded market where product quality is difficult to assess before purchase, the store's credibility signals do significant conversion work that lower-quality store designs cannot replicate.
