About mid/night 00.00

mid/night 00.00 is a genderless beauty brand built around a simple but powerful philosophy: advanced skincare formulas that contain only what your skin actually needs. Every product in their range is plant-based, vegan, and cruelty-free, designed for a modern consumer who reads ingredient labels and questions everything that goes on their skin.

The brand takes a minimalist, confident approach to its identity. The name itself, a reference to midnight and zero formulas, speaks to purity and precision. Their hero products include a Morning Parfum, a Face Oil, and a Hand Cream, all positioned at a premium price point that reflects the quality of the formulation and the sophistication of the brand story.

The Challenge They Brought to AspireNet

mid/night 00.00 came to AspireNet with a brand identity that was visually strong and philosophically compelling, but a Shopify store that was not communicating that quality to first-time visitors. The store felt generic in its layout and did not do justice to the premium positioning the brand had worked hard to establish.

The core challenge was translating a high-concept beauty brand into a digital shopping experience that felt as intentional and refined as the products themselves. Visitors needed to understand immediately what the brand stood for, trust the quality of the formulations, and find the path to purchase clear and frictionless.

Our Approach to the Shopify Build

AspireNet began with a thorough review of the brand guidelines, product photography, and target customer profile. The design direction we developed centred on a dark, editorial aesthetic that communicated luxury and confidence without relying on busy visual elements or heavy copy.

The homepage was structured to lead visitors through the brand story before presenting products. Rather than opening with a product grid, the store opens with the brand statement, supported by a strong hero image that establishes the visual language. Products are introduced with context, not just price and an add-to-cart button.

Product pages were given significant attention. Each product page communicates the formulation philosophy, the key ingredients, and the intended ritual of use. This matters for a premium skincare brand because the conversion decision in this category is built on trust and information, not impulse.

Technical Execution

The store was built on Shopify using a custom theme developed specifically for the mid/night 00.00 brand requirements. We implemented a multi-currency and multi-region setup to serve the European and international customer base the brand targets. The cart and checkout experience was streamlined to minimize friction at the point of purchase.

Page speed was a priority throughout the build. Premium brands often suffer from image-heavy stores that load slowly, undermining the quality perception they have worked hard to build. We implemented lazy loading, next-generation image formats, and optimized asset delivery to ensure fast load times without compromising visual quality.

The mobile experience was designed first and then adapted upward to desktop, reflecting the reality that the majority of beauty eCommerce traffic arrives on mobile devices.

The Outcome

The completed mid/night 00.00 Shopify store reflects the premium positioning of the brand in every detail. The design creates an immediate sense of sophistication that prepares the visitor to invest in a product before they have even read the first product description. The structured product pages build the trust and conviction required to convert a first-time visitor into a paying customer in a category where that trust is the primary purchase barrier.

The store serves customers across multiple European markets and represents a brand that is growing its presence in the premium clean beauty segment with a digital foundation built to support that ambition.
