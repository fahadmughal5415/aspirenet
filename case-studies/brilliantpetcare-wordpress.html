About Brilliant Petcare

Brilliant Petcare is a Norwegian pet wellness brand that produces premium salmon oil supplements for dogs and cats. Their flagship product, Brilliant Salmon Oil, is sourced and produced sustainably in Western Norway using traceable, high-quality salmon with a clear and verifiable supply chain. The product delivers a range of proven benefits for pets including a glossier coat, increased appetite, higher energy levels, and reduced skin itching and inflammation.

The brand's positioning is built around three words that appear consistently across all their communications: proven, traceable, and Norwegian. Each word carries specific meaning in the pet supplement market. Proven speaks to the documented benefits of omega-3 fatty acids for pets. Traceable speaks to the supply chain transparency that premium pet owners increasingly demand. Norwegian speaks to the quality and sustainability credentials of the Nordic aquaculture industry.

Selling Premium Pet Supplements Online

The pet supplement market has grown significantly but faces a persistent trust problem. Pet owners are buying products for animals they love deeply and cannot advocate for themselves. The quality variance in the supplement market is significant, ranging from genuinely beneficial products with traceable ingredients to low-quality formulations that deliver little nutritional value. Navigating this trust challenge is the central conversion problem for any premium pet supplement brand.

Brilliant Petcare needed a WordPress website that led with the Norwegian provenance and supply chain transparency that differentiates their product, communicated the specific benefits in language that resonates with pet owners rather than veterinary professionals, made the product range and subscription options easy to navigate, and built the trust required to justify a premium price in a category where cheaper alternatives are always one click away.

How AspireNet Designed the Website

The design approach for Brilliant Petcare was clean, warm, and trustworthy. The colour palette draws from the blues and whites of the Norwegian coastline, creating an immediate visual association between the brand and the premium Scandinavian quality credentials that underpin its positioning. Photography of the products alongside imagery of happy, healthy pets creates the emotional connection that drives purchase decisions in the pet care category.

The homepage was structured to open with the product benefit statement immediately, supported by the provenance story. Rather than leading with the company name and history, the site opens with what the product does for the pet and why Norwegian salmon oil specifically is the ingredient that delivers those results.

The traceability story was given dedicated space in the site architecture, because this is the specific differentiator that separates Brilliant Petcare from the majority of the pet supplement market. Pet owners who care about what they are feeding their animals will look for this information, and providing it clearly is both a conversion driver and a brand credibility signal.

WordPress Technical Build

Brilliant Petcare's WordPress website was built with WooCommerce powering the eCommerce functionality, with particular attention to the subscription and repeat purchase mechanics that are central to the business model. Pet supplements are a recurring purchase category, and the site was configured to make the subscribe and save option prominent and easy to manage for customers.

The product pages were built with a detailed information architecture that includes benefit descriptions, dosage guidance by pet size and weight, ingredient sourcing information, and customer review integration. This depth of information addresses the research behaviour of the premium pet supplement buyer, who will spend time on the product page before committing to a first purchase.

International shipping and multi-currency configuration was implemented to serve Brilliant Petcare's customer base across multiple markets, with appropriate tax and compliance handling for different regions.

What the Website Achieves

The Brilliant Petcare WordPress website gives the brand a digital home that does justice to the quality, transparency, and Norwegian provenance that define its market position. The site converts the trust that premium pet owners are actively looking for in the supplement category by making the traceability credentials visible and the product benefits specific and credible.

For a brand competing on quality and transparency in a market where those qualities are not universal, the website serves as the primary proof point that Brilliant Petcare is exactly what it claims to be.
