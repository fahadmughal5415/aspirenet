WHAT WE DO

Complete Local SEO Management

We handle every aspect of Local SEO so your business consistently appears at the top of local search results and Google Maps.

Google Business Profile Optimization

Complete GBP optimization — business categories, service areas, photos, posts, Q&A, and attributes — to maximize your visibility in the Google Local Pack.

Local Keyword Research

We identify the exact keywords your local customers use — "plumber near me," "best dentist in [city]" — and target them across your website and GBP.

Citation Building & Cleanup

We build consistent business listings (NAP) across 50+ directories — Yelp, Yellow Pages, Bing Places, Apple Maps — and fix any incorrect or duplicate listings.

Review Management

We create a review acquisition strategy to consistently grow your 5-star Google reviews — the #1 ranking factor in local search — and help respond to negative reviews professionally.

On-Page Local SEO

Location-specific page optimization, local schema markup, title tags, meta descriptions, and local content strategy to rank your website pages in local organic results.

Monthly Reporting

Clear monthly reports showing your keyword rankings, GBP insights (calls, directions, views), and overall progress — so you always know exactly what your investment is doing.
